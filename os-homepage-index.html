Why TerraOS

Landscape knowledge is fragmented.

Designers, contractors, suppliers, and owners each work from a different mental model of the same ground — different assumptions about light, soil, water, and what a plant actually needs to survive there.

Nothing connects what's known to what gets built, so knowledge gets recreated, lost, and disconnected from one stage to the next.

TerraOS is the shared intelligence layer that connects them — one system that carries a site's knowledge from the ground, through design, into what actually gets built and kept alive.
